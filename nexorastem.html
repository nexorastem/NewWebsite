Algorithmic '26

NexoraSTEM's flagship coding competition — held every three months — challenging students to solve algorithmic problems under time pressure. Compete, learn, and rise.

Competition Format

How it works

Three rounds of increasing difficulty. Individual scoring. Any approved programming language.

Round 1 · 90 min
Fundamentals
Arrays, strings, basic recursion.
Round 2 · 2 hrs
Intermediate Algorithms
Graphs, dynamic programming, binary search.
Round 3 · 3 hrs
Grand Finals
Advanced data structures, competitive-programming style. Top 8 proceed (as decided from previous two rounds).
